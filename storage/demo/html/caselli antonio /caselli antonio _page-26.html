I always had a passion for photography since my childhood. I started shooting photographs with my father s camera when I was only twelve, but I must confess that results were not at all encouraging. It was at university, when reading for my degree in archaeology that I re discovered my passion for photography as a mean of visual documentation. My love for archaeology and for the exotic brought me to discovery of foreign countries in Europe, North Africa and the Middle East a great opportunity to practice photography and develop my own technique which is continuosly evolving.
